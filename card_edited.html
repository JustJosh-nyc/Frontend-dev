Snow Leopard

They're well adapted to their cold environment.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera uncia
  • Order: Carnivora
  • Avg Weight: 39 - 51 inches
  • Region: Central and South Asia

Snow leopards are native to the mountainous regions of central and south asia. Around 60% of snow leopards are located in china. They are a nomadic and solitary breed that have have vocal cords similar to smaller cats so they can not roar. In the wild they live 10-13 years and in captivity can live up to 22 years. They are currently not endangered but are classified as vulnerable.
